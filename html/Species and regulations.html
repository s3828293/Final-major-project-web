Species and Regulations

An absolutely essential rule—except for exempt groups, all anglers must hold a valid Victorian fishing license to fish in Melbourne's waterways.

And Who is exempt?

  • You will not need a licence to fish if you are:
    • under 18 years of age;
    • 70 years of age or over;
  • or a holder of a:
    • Victorian Seniors Card or interstate equivalent;
    • Veterans' Affairs Pensioner Card;
    • Victorian Veterans Card;
    • Department of Veterans' Affairs Gold Card coded (TPI);
    • Commonwealth Pensioner Concession Card coded either (DSP), (DSP Blind), (AGE), (AGE Blind) or (CAR);
  • or:
    • A person that identifies as Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander
